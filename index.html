Plymtree Parish News

A monthly community newsletter for the Plymtree Parish. Run by volunteers. Proceeds go to the church. 10 issues at £6 for the whole year.

Subscribe

We publish 10 issues per year direct to your door (assuming you live locally) for £6. We encourage you to subscribe at stripe below for an automated subscription. Once you sign up, expect us to drop off the next issue at your door within the next month.

Contribute

We welcome all submissions from the community. If you have a story to share, an event to promote, or any content that you think would be of interest to our readers, please get in touch with us. We have everything from announcements of new babies, a kind word to a neighbour, a photograph of your pet, or a story about your garden. We are open to all contributions, and we encourage you to share your news with us. Please contact us via e-mail to submit your content, and we will be happy to include it in our next issue.
